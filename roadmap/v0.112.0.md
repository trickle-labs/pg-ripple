# v0.112.0 — A16 Critical & High Remediation + Dependency Maintenance

**Status:** ✅ Released — 2026-05-12

## Summary

v0.112.0 addresses the critical finding C16-01 (sixth-consecutive `COMPATIBLE_EXTENSION_MIN` lag)
and all four High-severity findings from Assessment 16, plus bumps `pg_trickle` to 0.57.0 and
`pg_tide` to 0.16.0. It also adds the v1.0.0 GA Entry Criteria section to the roadmap.
